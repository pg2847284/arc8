FAQs

What is the Arc8 GameFest?

The Arc8 GameFest is a premier Web3 gaming event taking place within the Arc8 mobile gaming application, created by GAMEE, a subsidiary of Animoca Brands. Players can compete for a share of an extensive prize pool, consisting of tokens and NFTs.

When does the Arc8 GameFest take place?

The event is scheduled from November 16-30. You can join at any point during the event, though there will be a cooldown until the following day (beginning at 13:00 UTC). After then, you will be able to play with your newly purchased Arc8 GameFest Pass.

How do I participate in the Arc8 GameFest?

To participate, purchase a GameFest Pass, connect and pair your wallet on Arc8.com, and download the free Arc8 app.

How are the daily token prizes distributed?

Players are randomly placed in a group of 50 players every day (from 13:00 UTC). Players who finish in the top 20% of their group at the end of the day (13:00 UTC) are each allocated an equal share of the rewards.

Do I need to be an expert gamer to win prizes?

No, the Arc8 GameFest is designed for players of all skill levels. Player pools are limited to 50 people and are refreshed daily, offering everyone a chance to win in Daily Rounds.

What is the Elite Arcade?

The Elite Arcade is a special zone within the Arc8 GameFest, accessible only to those who own more than one GameFest Pass. Within this zone, players can compete for a dedicated $20,000 reward pool in gated competitions.

Where can I download the Arc8 app?

The Arc8 app is available for free on both the App Store and Google Play .

How can I contact support if I have any issues?

Ways to contact our support team can be found on our wiki .

What games can I play during the GameFest?

Arc8 offers a variety of thrilling games, including the newly introduced live multiplayer games, Disc-O Mania and Cranky Monkey. It’s entirely up to you which games you play. There are also sports games, card games, action games and mind-bending puzzles.
